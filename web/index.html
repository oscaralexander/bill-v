Questions we ask

If you have at least one solid answer in the following buckets, Bill is interested:

  • Can you build/create it?

    Bill likes founders and teams that can create the product and take it to market.

  • Can you find customers?

    We look for evidence of at least one repeatable channel to acquire customers (no friends and family :))

  • Are people/businesses willing to pay for it?

    Ideally you already have a couple (and growing number) of paying customers that pay a reasonable amount for your product.

  • Can your business fly?

    We like businesses that are scalable by design.

  • Can you enthuse people around you?

    What is the reason people would like to work for your company? Or become a partner? Or a client?
